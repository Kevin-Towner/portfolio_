NJ Transit Mobile App - Redesign

Introduction

Many would agree with the statement, "commuting via public transportation is less stressful than commuting by personal vehicle". Despite having more direct control over the route and speed, many commuters and travelers like public transportation. The ability to be productive on the train or to take a nap the bus are nice features of public transportation. Many experts also believe public transportation is more environmentally sustainable.

The Challenge

Nj Transit's mobile application has trip planning functionality, but there are many areas of opportunity to improve upon this function.

There are standard interactions and user inputs such as the buttons and forms. The application will respond to the user's parameters and give the available options (from the time the users finished their parameters).

Once, the users pick their options, they are shown a mini agenda, which includes the train fairs, the origin stations, and the destination stations.

Goal

The goal is to develop a new user interface and add more functionality to the existing application. The redesigned NJ Transit app will allow the users to input their origin stations, destination stations and final destinations. There will also be the ability to purchase tickets, receive push notifications, save tickets, save trips, and receive GPS navigation for the users to find the best route towards their final destinations.
