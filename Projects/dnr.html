About the Dashboard

The Wire is probably my favorite television series of all time. David Simon, the show's writer, did a great job of building the characters in a way that tells the story without relying on direct dialogue or musical cutscenes. It's one of those shows that the viewers forget is a work of fiction. With subject-matter experts having been involved in the writing and character development of The Wire, the TV series serves as a great character study; providing individual motivations, pain-points and behaviors within the Baltmore Police Department.

Personas from The Wire

Using the events, chracters and storyline from The Wire I created three archetypes within Baltimore's Police Department. Each of the characters representing the arcehtypes has his or her own behaviors, painpoints and motivations towards pursuing the investigation's main targets, Avon Barksdale and Stringer Bell. Additionally, each character would have good reason to use a digital dashboard and spend the majority of their day in-house vs on the street.
