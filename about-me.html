Professional

I'm a digital product designer with an interest in building intuitive user-interface components. I believe in advocating for the user, by taking a human-centered approach, and streamlining the user experience. My main design tools include Sketch, Invision, HTML & CSS.

During my 9-5, I am an account analyst at AIG insurance where I serve as a cross-functional ops team member. My day job entails client communication and data analysis, with the purpose of driving productivity across our many software platforms.

I enjoy utilizing industry-standard tools and collaboration to identify objectives and solve problems.
